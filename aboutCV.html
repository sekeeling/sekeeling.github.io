Sarah Keeling
Sarah Keeling is a multidisciplinary artist whose work considers the landscape, both natural and built. She looks at design as a set of instructions and creates intuitive responses through photography, video, sculpture and public works. The images and objects she creates combine familiarity and humor to make comedic yet contemplative works. She currently resides in Pittsburgh PA and holds a BFA from Carnegie Mellon University.

Her work has been exhibited at ISEA2015 Vancouver, Canada; Bronco Gallery Portland, OR; the Queens Museum, Queen, NY; the Visual Studies Gallery, University at Buffalo, Buffalo, NY; 973 Liberty Avenue, Pittsburgh, PA; the Billboard Project and the West Virginia University Arts Museum & Educational Center.
