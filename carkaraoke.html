Car Karaoke is a participatory event featured in the 2013 Free City Public Art Festival organized by the Flint Public Art Project in Flint, MI. Through using an adapted karaoke system, festival visitors were invited to sing songs to each other from separate vehicles. The night time performances were recorded and then replayed in the two silver sedans during the day.

Materials: two Chevrolet sedans, PA system, microphones, portable projection screen, projector and lights. (2013)
